What Will You Get From This Book?

In "Ayurvedic 101," you'll gain practical insights into Ayurvedic principles, herbal remedies, dietary guidelines, and self-care practices. Ideal for BAMS students, medical beginners, and anyone interested in natural health practices, this book is your guide to holistic well-being.

Introduction to Ayurvedic Principles

Understand the fundamental concepts of Ayurveda, including the five elements and the three doshas (Vata, Pitta, and Kapha), and how they influence your health and well-being.

Personalized Health Insights

Learn how to identify your unique dosha and tailor your lifestyle, diet, and self-care practices to achieve balance and harmony.

Herbal Remedies and Natural Healing

Discover the healing properties of common Ayurvedic herbs and how to use them to address various health issues naturally.

Ayurvedic Dietary Guidelines

Explore the importance of diet in Ayurveda and how to make food choices that support your dosha and promote overall health.

Daily and Seasonal Routines

Gain practical tips on establishing daily routines (dinacharya) and seasonal routines (ritucharya) that align with Ayurvedic principles for optimal health.

Self-Care Practices

Learn about Ayurvedic self-care techniques, including massage (abhyanga), detoxification (panchakarma), and mindfulness practices to enhance your physical and mental well-being.
